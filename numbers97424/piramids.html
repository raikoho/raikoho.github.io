What Is Numerology?

Numerology is the study of numbers as they relate to our lives, events, and the cosmos. Each number carries unique vibrations and meanings that can reveal hidden truths about ourselves and the universe.

The Core Numbers

In numerology, there are five core numbers derived from your name and birthdate:

  • Life Path Number: Your life's journey and purpose.
  • Expression Number: Your talents and abilities.
  • Soul Urge Number: Your deepest desires.
  • Personality Number: How others perceive you.
  • Birth Day Number: Unique traits you bring to the world.

How to Calculate Your Life Path Number

To calculate your Life Path Number, sum the digits of your full birthdate until you reach a single digit:

Example: If your birthdate is July 14, 1992:

  • 7 (July) + 1 + 4 (Day) + 1 + 9 + 9 + 2 (Year) = 33
  • 3 + 3 = 6

Your Life Path Number is 6, which symbolizes nurturing, responsibility, and harmony.

Discover Hidden Patterns

Numerology isn't just about single numbers. Combinations, sequences, and patterns all play a role in understanding the forces shaping your life. Keep an eye out for repeating numbers like 111, 222, or 888—they are signs from the Universe!
