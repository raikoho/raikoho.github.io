MYKOLA Tkachuk - THE GLOWING MADNESS

Biography

Mykola Tkachuk is the essence of chaos. His ideas, as unpredictable as the weather, were often described as 'too far-reaching'. His game, intended to bring forth the secrets of the universe, was perpetually delayed due to his obsessive commitment to reading, gaming, and finding the perfect shade of pink for his room.

Endless Repetition

Mykola’s approach to life was like his approach to programming—repetitive, inefficient, and yet oddly captivating. His game, much like his everyday actions, kept going in circles, never quite reaching its destination. But in the end, perhaps it was the repetition that gave him the most joy. Who needs progress when you can repeat everything a thousand times?
